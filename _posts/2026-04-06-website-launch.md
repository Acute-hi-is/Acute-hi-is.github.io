We're excited to announce the launch of the new ACUTE Lab website. The site has been redesigned from the ground up to better reflect the breadth and depth of our research across vibrotactile perception, spatial audio, and acoustic diagnostics.

The new site features detailed research area pages, individual team member profiles, a filterable publications archive, and project descriptions — all built as a fast, accessible static site.

We hope the site makes it easier for collaborators, students, and the wider research community to explore our work and get in touch.
